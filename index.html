Medical insurance cost prediction

In this project, the cost of the medical insurance was predicted using the “smoker” variable. Linear regression was used as machine learning algorithm. R2 coefficient was used as the evaluation metric. An adjustment of 60% was obtained. There were several variables involved. Besides, the data comes from United States.
