Interests


Artificial Intelligence

It is a discipline which tries to replicate the human intelligence on machines. Its scope includes machine learning and deep learning. The former uses statistical methods to enable machines to improve with experience, it works fine with structured data. The latter makes the computation of multi-layer neural network feasible. It works fine with unstructured data such as images, text and within others. Generative AI is another new field of AI which aims to create new content rather than just analyzing or interpreting it.

Predictive models

These are algorithms that are trained in historical data to make predictions about future events or outcomes. These models analyze patterns and relationships within the data to generate predictions or probabilities for new data points. Predictive models are used in a wide range of applications, including sales forescasting, risk assessment, fraud detection, healthcare diagnosis, customer churn prediction and many others. They can help organizations make informed decisions, optimize processes, and improve outcomes.

Big Data

The field of Big Data have increasing its presence and utility in the project of understanding how the data-driven decisions can come from the great amount of sources such as social media. The analysis of Big Data offers significant opportunities for businesses and organizations in various sectors. It can enable them to gain insights, make data-driven decisions. However, managing and extracting value from Big Data also presents challenges related to data quality, security, privacy, and the need for specialized skills and infrastructure.
